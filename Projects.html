Vench Club Personal Project

Vench Club was a clothing brand I started during the pandemic lockdown. The goal of this brand was to highlight City Heights, San Diego as it is one of the most diverse communities of San Diego. City Heights is also home to immigrants and I wanted to showcase that even though City Heights is often overlooked when people think of San Diego, It is a community that contirbutes to being part of America's Finest City. To showcase the beauty of City Heights, I created a mini documentary that reached almost 30k views across all social media platforms.
